Shakshuka (Eggs Poached in Spicy Tomato Sauce – Morocco)

fresh tomatoes stewed in olive oil, cumin and smoked paprika.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped/li>
  • 1 red pepper, chopped/li>
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, chopped (optional)
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 cup fresh oregano, chopped
  • 4-6 eggs
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a medium to large skillet.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic for 1 minute.
  3. Add red pepper. Sauté for a few minutes.
  4. Add jalapeno pepper. Sauté for about 30 seconds.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, cumin and smoked paprika. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until sauce begins to thicken. Salt and pepper, to taste.
  6. Stir in oregano.
  7. Crack eggs on top of mixture. Continue to simmer for a few minutes until egg begins to set.
  8. Stir eggs into mixture. Turn off heat. Salt and pepper, to taste.
